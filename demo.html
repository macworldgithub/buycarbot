Buy My Next Car — Chat Widget Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable chat widget loaded from the live deployment at buycarbot.omnisuiteai.com. Click the teal button in the bottom-right corner to open it.

To embed this on any site, add this before </body>: 

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://buycarbot.omnisuiteai.com/iife.css">
<script src="https://buycarbot.omnisuiteai.com/iife.js"
        data-api-url="https://buycarbot.omnisuiteai.com"></script>

That's it — no other setup needed on the embedding site.
